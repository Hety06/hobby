Photoshopping Images Together

When I want to create a photoshop, I like to just brainstorm about what I should make. Sometimes I just want to combine two animals. Animal combinations are always the best ones to do because if you find animals with similar colors, it will look really good. After choosing the animals I want, I just search for animals with similar colors. If you're photoshop is lacking something, you can always add filters, my favorite is Oil Paint.

The Process

First I open one of the pictures on photoshop, usually the one I like the background on more. After that I open the other animal and use the cut tool to cut out what I want to add. Then I copy the image I want to cut out and paste it on the first opened picture. Once I've lined up the images I use the eraser tool to cut off any excess. Afterwards I like to use the blur and smudge tools to merge the images together. Maybe change the opacity of the images to make them merge easier.
